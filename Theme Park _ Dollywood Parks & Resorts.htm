Dollywood Theme Park

Incredible Experience... Authentic Fun... Unforgettable Memories... Crafted Daily.

Spanning 150 acres in the Great Smoky Mountains, Dollywood offers more than 40 world class rides, high-energy entertainment, award-winning dining and the friendliest park atmosphere in the world! We even received an award for being so friendly!
