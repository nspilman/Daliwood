Plan Your Visit

The Great Smoky Mountains can make you feel right at home in the same way it calls Dolly Parton home. With so much to do and so much to see, it is no wonder why Dollywood Parks & Resorts is truly someplace special.

Getting To Dollywood

Wait, How Close Am I?

Dollywood is conveniently located 35 miles southeast of Knoxville, Tennessee in scenic Pigeon Forge at the gateway of the Great Smoky Mountains National Park. Make yourself right at home, even before you arrive!

The Dollywood Experience

More than 50 rides and attractions, world-class entertainment, one-of-a-kind dining and a new reason to visit every season. Use our Trip Planner to plan your visit before you arrive! Just hit the star symbol to add items to your itinerary.

Our full-service and buffet-style restaurants are perfect for meals together as a family. Or, pick up something on-the-go at any quick-service concession stand for a snack break during the fun. Our world-famous cinnamon bread is a sweet finish (or start!) to your visit.

Lodging Options

Looking for the complete family getaway in the Smoky Mountains? Dollywood's DreamMore Resort & Spa and Dollywood's Smoky Mountain Cabins offer the perfect opportunities for families to reconnect.

DreamMore Resort & Spa

Centered on the rich traditions of storytelling, family and togetherness, the resort features many special touches including family sanctuaries like fire pits, swings and hammocks plus story spots scattered throughout the grounds.
